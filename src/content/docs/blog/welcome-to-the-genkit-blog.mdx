Welcome to the official Genkit blog. This is where we'll share product
announcements, engineering deep dives, and practical patterns for building
AI-powered applications.

Posts here are authored in **MDX**, which means we can mix regular Markdown
with the same interactive components used throughout the docs.

<Aside type="tip">
  Because posts are MDX, you can drop in any Starlight component, like this
  aside, right alongside your prose.
</Aside>
